💡 How It Works

The recommendation system analyzes each route and calculates a wind score based on wind conditions along the entire route. Routes with tailwinds in the second half receive 70% of the total score weight, because it's better to fight headwinds when you're fresh and enjoy tailwinds when you're tired on the way back. Try different wind directions to see how the rankings change!
